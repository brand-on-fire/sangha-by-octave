Home, is the source where we nurture healthy relationships. Our first life community, where we seek love, safety and shelter. Home is a relationship, it is a place of love; a culture; a life journey; a responsibility. The quality of our relationships, defines the quality of our lives.

Relationship develops from within and expresses itself externally. If home is like a garden, the gardener is the hand that sows and nurtures the seed. As adults, you are the gardener. You were once being nurtured, now you are the hands that nurture. Family learning is a continuous journey of learning life and life learning. It is adults learning how to become the loving hands that nurture a flourishing garden.
